What we know so far about Shadow worm The Shadow Worm, known scientifically as Umbra Serpens, is shrouded in mystery and legend. It has been observed gliding effortlessly through the dark dimensions, an elusive entity that defies the laws of nature as we understand them. Its massive size, comparable to a small mountain, allows it to dominate the shadowy landscape, often mistaken for the shifting sands of its environment.
